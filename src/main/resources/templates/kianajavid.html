PROFESSIONAL PROJECTS

Markis Shopping Center



Designed by Markis Group, The Spot is a new commercial building in Tehran’s Grand Bazaar that brings residences to an area surrounded by 72 boutiques. In this project I was in charge of designing a 4-storey commercial building. Having an effective communication plays a vital role in this project due to the limitation of the site. As a junior architect of a design team, I was responsible for preparing drawings using AutoCAD and Revit, and developing renderings.




Javid Restaurant



Javid Restaurant was designed by Markis Company, it is a 4-storey building with capacity of 300 people located in Grand Bazaar Tehran. As an architect, I was responsible to design this restaurant based on the client’s need using autoCAD, Revit and preparing the 3D design by collaborating with design and engineering team. Having an effective communication with client was a key of success to achieve his desired design. I was also in charge of designing furniture, interior using autoCAD, Revit and executing renderings using 3D Max.




Nabi Apartment



Nabi Apartment was designed and renovated by Markis Company. It was a duplex penthouse located in Tehran. As my first experience as an architect intern, my duty was preparing architectural drawings using AutoCAD, and providing design concepts to senior architect. I also assist in designing the interior of building and travel to China to help the client to get his desired furniture based on our design.
